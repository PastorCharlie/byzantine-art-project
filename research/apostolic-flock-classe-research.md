- **Three theological points** developed in the reflection:
  1. **Uniformity** — the twelve lambs are visually identical; no Petrine supremacy, no apostolic hierarchy. *"There is neither Greek nor Jew... but Christ is all, and in all"* (Col 3:11).
  2. **Flock metaphor literalized** — the apostles ARE Christ's sheep (John 10:27); they are the first generation of the flock, not a separate class above it.
  3. **Apollinaris among them** — the bishop is shown standing *in* the flock, not above it. Pastoral office model: local successor stands among the sheep, leading their prayer, not standing apart.
