- **Object:** Bema arch mosaics flanking the apse Theotokos (#25). Gabriel south side preserved, Michael north side largely destroyed. c. 867+ post-iconoclasm program.

## Calibration (Coll 8 framing-rule first execution)
- **Named-archangels canonicity rule applied:** Michael and Gabriel canonical (Daniel, Jude, Revelation, Luke); Raphael (Tobit), Uriel (2 Esdras), and the seven-archangels Eastern tradition declined as deuterocanonical/extra-canonical.
- **Ministering-not-mediating articulated:** the bema-arch composition has the archangels in *court attendance* posture (orbs and staffs, court garments not warrior armor), framing the apse Theotokos. The architecture stages angelic ministry — they attend the throne; they do not mediate between humans and God.
- **Veneration-rejection cited via Col 2:18 + Rev 22:8–9** — the strongest biblical foundation against angel-worship.
- **Cross-tag iconoclasm-debate:** post-iconoclasm program (867 dated), important to Coll 10 cross-tagging.
